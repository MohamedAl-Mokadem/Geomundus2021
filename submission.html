2020 Papers Submission

The submission for short papers and posters has closed.

The best paper award for the short paper submissions in 2020 were provided to the following students:

  • Elias Nasr Naim Elias, Fabricio Rosa Amorim, Marcio Augusto Reolon Schmidt and Silvana Philippi Camboim (Federal University of Paraná) for the paper Data quality analysis tools and their use for the detection of spatiotemporal heterogeneities in VGI contributions.
  • Alba Vilanova Cortezón (NOVA University of Lisbon, Information Management School) for the paper Wind Energy Assessment in Costa Rica using Multicriteria Evaluation.
  • Vince Kenneth M. del Mundo (University of the Philippines) for the paper Earthquake Deformation Mapping caused by the Taal Volcano Eruption using InSAR.
Congratulations!

For any questions about templates and formats please send us an email to info@geomundus.org

Selected authors will be notified by email and accepted short papers and posters will be displayed on the GeoMundus website. The paper presentation will be 10 minutes long, followed by a 5 minutes question-answer session. The official language of the conference is English.

Eligibility: Currently enrolled student at a university, graduated in 2020 or enrolling in 2020 (undergraduate, masters, doctorate).

GRANTS

With Geomundus 2020 shifting to a digital conference, travel grants will NOT be possible for paper submissions. Considering this update, the 3 best paper submissions will now have prizes in the form of €250 gift cards from PayPal’s gift card section.

*Applicants must check to see if PayPal gift cards are available for use in their respective locations. Geomundus 2020 will not be responsible for unredeemed gift cards and grants cannot be reimbursed in cash.
